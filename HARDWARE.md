# Hardware Documentation

MIDI companion controller with two analog wheels (pitch bend + mod wheel), two buttons, a footswitch, two LEDs, and a 128x64 OLED display. Two hardware implementations exist.

See the implementation-specific files:

- [HARDWARE_TEENSY.md](HARDWARE_TEENSY.md) — Teensy 3.2 (`src/main.cpp`)
- [HARDWARE_PICO.md](HARDWARE_PICO.md) — Raspberry Pi Pico 2 (`circuitpython/`)

Fritzing schematic (Teensy): `midicompanion2-0.fzz`
